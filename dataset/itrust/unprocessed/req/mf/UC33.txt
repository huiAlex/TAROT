A sending HCP refers a patient to another receiving HCP [S1]. A receiving HCP views a list of received referrals [S2]. A sending HCP views a list of previously sent patient referrals [S3]. A patient views the details of his/her referrals [S4]. A sending HCP edits a previously sent patient referral [S5]. A sending HCP cancels a previously sent patient referral [S6]. All events are logged (UC5).
